Simon Says

Here is a little javascript application of the classic game of Simon. I used javascript, SVG (created in Adobe Illustrator), and JQuery to create this little thing.

    So here are the rules:
  • The game starts with just 2 lights to memorize.
  • Each level adds 2 more lights to remember until level 10 then each level only adds 1 more light.
  • Each round has a 2 minute limit up to level 15 and then 3 minutes after that to prevent stagnant games.
  • The game only has 30 levels. That is a total of 40 lights.

Just press the round red button when you are ready to play. Press the round yellow button to pause the timer at anytime to pause the game. Press the round red button to start the timer again. Pressing the red round button will replay the last sequence and then you know the rest. Have fun!
